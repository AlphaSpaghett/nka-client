Norwalk Karting Association

Founded in 1971, Norwalk Karting Association has been a long standing tradition in Norwalk, CT. We race on Saturdays in the spring and the fall, and have competitors ranging from 5 - 70. Many famous racecar drivers have gotten their start in motor racing at "the beach". These include Scott Sharp, Parker Kligerman, Todd Szegedy, Jerry Nadeau, Randy Lajoie, Ron Silk, Scott Heckert and others. Come find out what racing is all about in a fun, affordable, and family friendly atmosphere!
